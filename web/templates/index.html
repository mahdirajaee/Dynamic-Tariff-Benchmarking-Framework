Optimization Technology

Our framework uses state-of-the-art mixed-integer linear programming (MILP) to jointly optimize:

  • Load shifting and demand response
  • Battery charging and discharging
  • Peer-to-peer energy trading
  • Grid import/export decisions

Research Objectives

This framework addresses the challenge of minimizing electricity costs in prosumer communities while maintaining equitable cost distribution across all participants.

Primary Goal

Minimize aggregate electricity cost of all prosumer buildings through optimal tariff selection and P2P trading configuration.

Fairness Focus

Ensure equitable cost savings distribution using comprehensive fairness metrics and Coefficient of Variation analysis.
